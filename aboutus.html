LEADERSHIP

SURI GOPALAN, FOUNDER

Over 30 years of Media Distribution experience for more information

Suri has played an active part in the Indian film industry and was instrumental in the physical sales of Indian compact discs and video in the United States, through Raaga Music Superstores, his entrepreneurial venture.

Suri also produced the first show of multi-platinum Indian musician, A.R Rahman, in conjunction with Rapport Entertainment of Bombay. The show was a huge success and was seen by over 50,000 people at the Shrine auditorium in Los Angeles and the Cow Palace in San Francisco. A.R Rahman went on to win two Oscars in 2010. Suri holds an MA in International Business from the Columbia University - School of International and Public Affairs

RAJIV RAGHUNATHAN CEO

Rajiv comes with over 25 years of hands-on experience in film production, post-production and digital distribution. He has successfully worked on over 350 feature films, 100 commercials, 30 Audio Visuals.

Prior to joining Vista India, Rajiv headed the Post Production Unit at Reliance Media Works and was also associated with Prasad EFX. He was an entrepreneur for 7 years and ran a successful Advertising Film Production house. He led the post-production team which won several popular awards for the film "Krrish", starring Hrithik Roshan and "Dil To Pagal Hai", starring Shah Rukh Khan.

Rajiv has had the experience, exposure and privilege to work on some of the finest advertising commercials, breath-taking documentaries, high profile Corporate Audio-Visuals and the biggest Bollywood flicks.

He brings along with him strong skill sets like film production and post, digital distribution, people management, strong business acumen, relationship management, customer focus, analytical expertise and risk assessment skills along with a comprehensive understanding of business processes, industry and competition.

RAVI GOPALAN

DIRECTOR-CFO

A Qualified Chartered Accountant and a Business graduate from St Xavier’s Calcutta and an Accredited Jewelry Professional from the Gemological Institute of America.

Ravi Gopalan is the Co-founder with Suri Gopalan and the Director-CFO at Vista India. He is an active consultant for the Mining, Metals and Energy Industries specializing in cross border transactions. With an experience in the Corporate Finance and Business Strategy, he also has immense knowledge in the Diamond and Jewelry Manufacturing business.

He has an additional experience in Minority Affairs with focus on Economic and Community Development. An elected District Delegate for the National Democratic Convention 2004 and a Board Member of the Asian Indian Chamber of Commerce, he is fluent in many South Asian languages.
