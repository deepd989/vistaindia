Who We Are

Vista India specializes in global digital distribution of movies, documentaries and TV shows. We provide a seamless distribution service to Netflix, Amazon, Google Play, Apple TV and other premium platforms.

Our mission is to present your film to the largest audience possible. We have delivered large number of films for major Indian studios on these platforms. We support independent filmmakers with digital distribution of their films worldwide.

We provide a highly sophisticated automated dashboard where you can track and monitor the revenue earned by your film on a daily basis. Our services are used by most of the leading studios, broadcasters and independents in this market.

What We Do

What We Do

We work with some of the largest Indian studios and broadcasters and assist in getting your film featured prominently on our partner platform. Our clients include Red Chillies, Sun Network, Viacom18, Balaji Motion Pictures, Disney UTV, among others.

Pioneers in taking regional films on OTT platforms, we have successfully delivered content in Indian languages like Marathi, Tamil, Telugu, Malayalam, Bengali, Kannada, Punjabi, Oriya, Assamese and Gujarati across all our digital platforms.

SUPPLY CHAIN

We understand digital and the global supply chain issues or challenges that are specific to Indian content and marketplace. We go a step above pure play aggregation by actively creating workflows and systems that are user friendly. Our objective is to create an honest and transparent organization committed to helping you build value for your content worldwide. We provide state-of-the-art tools that manage, share, process, interface, compute, analyze, and report the data and other details you require.

LOCALIZATION SERVICES

Our experienced team of linguists delivers several 100 hours to premium OTT platforms every month. We specialize in Indian languages like Hindi, Marathi, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Malayalam, Bengali, Oriya, Assamese, Gujarati and Punjabi. We offer localization services not just for Indian content but also for content originating from different parts of the world. Similarly, with our worldwide network of writers and translators we can offer our localization services for all languages spoken and read globally.

CLIENT DASHBOARD & ANALYTICS

We have a state-of-the-art dashboard where content owners have access to daily sales, reports analytics, accounts and other status reports. Our dashboard is specifically designed to provide you detailed and meticulous reports on all your expenses and provides unmatchable transparency. We are constantly upgrading our dashboard to provide you with a superior level of visibility and value addition.

CUSTOMER SERVICE

There is nothing greater than having all your requirements fulfilled in time. One of our goals is to provide the best level of service that you would expect from your own office. Our philosophy is to offer outstanding service and to be always available.
