Audio Visual QC

We employ a combination of automated and manual QC. We hold the expertise and tools for repairs and fixes in-house such as pillar boxing, interlacing, video hits, audio drop-outs, and much more. Our experienced QC technicians are set by premium OTT platforms and can seamlessly handle the deliveries. We have been awarded the prestigious status of Netflix Preferred Fulfillment Partner and Apple Approved Encoding House.

Key Services

  • Full QC – Visual and Automated
  • HDR QC and Delivery
  • Digitization
  • Transcoding
  • Spot QC and Report Generation
  • Censor / Compliance Edits
  • IMF Packaging

Metadata

We offer a wide range of metadata services to ensure easy discovery of your content in the respective OTT platform. We have our own tool for metadata creation, which compiles information like synopsis, chapter images, film credits, copyright line, etc. A dedicated team of experienced professionals is available to our clients to check the metadata and ensure the same is within the framework of OTT platforms. We go one step further and also localize the metadata for various territories.

Key Services

  • Metadata Creation
  • Metadata QC/Conformance
  • Metadata Localization
